About Me


Hi! I'm Nala

Writer, Traveller, Photographer, Lover

I was born and raised in the lovely city of Hong Kong. I am a translator who specialises
in Japanese and English, so I am blessed with the many opportunities to voyage in
Japan. Join me as I document my ventures in the Land of the Rising Sun! My goal is to
visit every region in Japan. So far, I've covered Hokkaido, Tohoku, Kanto, Chubu,
Kansai, Kyushu, and Okinawa. My favourite location is Okinawa, where the beautiful
supple sand meets the clear turquoise sea.
